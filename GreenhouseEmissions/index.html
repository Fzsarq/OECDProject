OECD

The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development helps governments around the world "foster prosperity and fight poverty through economic growth and financial stability". Rich in history and strong in numbers, they aim to promote economic and environmental sustainability. Data on all countries is collected and analyzed to determine a course of action which will help build a stronger foundation which will promote an even stronger future. The data collected by the OECD includes more than just economic growth. It collects environmental information such as climate, environmental waste and water over a long period of time and analyzes the fluctuations of the data. Ultimately, theses fluctuations can be linked to the emissions of Greenhouse gases. This website aims to present the information collected in a easy to understand format so that the general public will understand the implications that their habits have on the environment, and start developing ways to protect the future of this planet.
